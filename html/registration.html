Venue & Location

Conference Venue

John Paul II Center "Do Not Be Afraid"

ul. Totus Tuus, 30-001 Kraków, Poland

Modern conference facilities located in Kraków, the city closely connected with the life and ministry of Saint John Paul II, with convenient access to major churches, historical sites, and public transportation.

Facilities:
  • Multiple conference rooms and chapels
  • High-speed Wi-Fi
  • Audio-visual equipment
  • Accessible facilities

Transportation

From Airport

Kraków John Paul II International Airport (KRK)

  • Train to city center: ~20 min
  • Taxi: ~30 min
  • Airport shuttle services

Public Transit

  • Trams and buses throughout the city
  • Easy access to Old Town and Wawel
  • Single ride tickets and day passes available
  • Contactless payment on many lines

Other Options

  • Taxi and ride-sharing services
  • Bike and electric scooter rentals
  • Many sites within walking distance
  • Limited parking at the venue
